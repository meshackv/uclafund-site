Hi Bruins,
Are you ready to start your weekend with a blue and gold twist? The fifth annual Blue & Gold Challenge begins today, and the entire campus is feeling the excitement.
As a Chancellor’s Society donor at UCLA, your leadership sets the example for annual giving each year. Our Blue & Gold Challenge is no exception. We rely on your leadership and support to help us build momentum toward our 3,500-gift goal, and toward unlocking this year’s record-setting $500,000 challenge gift to the university.

JOIN THE CHALLENGE

This year’s challenge gift comes from the generous family of Gigi and Craig Barto. You can read more about these fellow Chancellor’s Society donors on our Blue & Gold Challenge website.
We are grateful for your generous leadership support and ask that you make a participation gift this week as a show of support for our entire UCLA community uniting during this unique time. If November is that time you usually renew your leadership gift, please consider doing so during the Challenge to enhance your gift’s impact. With a record-breaking challenge gift at stake, your leadership and support with an additional gift of any size is invaluable to our success as one UCLA community.
Thank you for your commitment to UCLA.
GO BRUINS!
Jay Palchikoff ’77, JD ’82, UCLA Parent
Chair, UCLA Fund
